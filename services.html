Why an indoor tracking system?

Indoor geolocation is a technique for locating objects in industrial buildings, such as factories or warehouses.

This information can be used by production managers to track production flows, or by warehouse workers to locate lost items.

Indoor geolocation is the solution for environments where GPS reception is not available.

SIGSCAN is the affordable indoor geolocation solution based on Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology.

SIGSCAN technology

SIGSCAN is based on Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology.

BLE is the low-energy version of Bluetooth. Thanks to BLE, our beacons, equipped with a flat battery, have a lifespan of 3 years and a range of 50 meters.

BLE, and therefore SIGSCAN, is compatible with all Bluetooth-enabled smartphones, tablets and other devices.

The antennas we install in your buildings calculate the positions of the beacons and send them via Wifi or Ethernet to the SIGSCAN server installed locally in the building or in our cloud.

The antennas cover areas of up to 50 meters.

Thanks to our mobile and web applications, you can visualize the position of your objects and even extract movement histories.

Gateway antenna

⦁ Bluetooth “beacons” are geolocation systems: they are available on the market in various formats.
⦁ They are self-contained and run on replaceable or non-replaceable batteries (depending on the model) with a life of up to 3 years (depending on use).
⦁ Bluetooth “Gateways” antennas, to be installed at strategic points in a building, which receive Bluetooth signals and retransmit them via Wifi/Ethernet/CPL to a dedicated server (Data Retriever) installed at the Customer's site or on the Pyrénées Software Cloud.
⦁ A “Data Retriever” calculation server processing the Bluetooth signals transmitted to it and calculating the positions of the beacons.
⦁ A central “Back-End” database, in charge of storing all data, object positions, system configuration...
⦁ A “Front-End” Web interface enabling users to visually geolocate the objects they are looking for on a map of the building from their computer.
⦁ An “Android / iOS” Mobile application allowing a user to search for an object in the field with their phone or tablet.

A wide range of applications

Logistics warehouses

In a logistics warehouse, SIGSCAN enables :
- Know the position of your parcels and materials to be stored, all in 3D.
- Reduce search times
- Study parcel movements by type or overall
- Finally, optimizing organization and improving processes

Production plants

In a manufacturing plant, SIGSCAN will enable :
- Track and locate parts during production (production orders))
- Send alerts when anomalies are detected, such as an object stagnating for too long or moving to an unexpected area
- Study manufacturing processes (monitoring routings), and ultimately improve productivity

To facilitate logistics and quality monitoring, SIGSCAN has devised a system for locating a range of objects within a building. Bluetooth technology makes this solution customizable and accessible to the widest possible audience.
